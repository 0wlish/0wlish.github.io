Passenger Pigeon

Gouache on paper depicting a Passenger Pigeon sitting on a telephone wire as a jet airliner passes overhead. Note that this is an impossible scene, as the pigeon is thought to have gone extinct in the wild in 1900, and the first commercial jet airliner flight happened in 1952. 2024.
